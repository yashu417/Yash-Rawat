BALENO

TECH GOES BOLD

Maruti Suzuki is gearing up to launch the facelifted version of its popular premium hatchback, Baleno.First launched in 2015, this will The all new baleno costs between Rs 6.35 lakh to Rs 9.49 lakh(ex-showroom,India) comes with the bold, sharp and edgy looks. It's a full model generation change with a complete overhaul of the previous Baleno, featuring exterior and interior makeovers and significant technological upgrades, it looks more more stylish and sharper and feels plusher and premium than its predecessors.

2022 Baleno is offered in Sigma, Delta, Zeta and Alpha variants. The new Baleno will go up against the likes of Hyundai i20, Tata Altroz, VW Polo, Citreon C3, Honda Jazz and facelifted toyota glanza. Interestingly, Maruti Suzuki has continued to leave out a sunroof from its offerings. Maruti Suzuki also plans to launch 7 New facelift models during 2022, with the first being the new 2022 Baleno.
